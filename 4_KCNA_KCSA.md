This is two certifications that focus on **theoretical** knowledge of Kubernetes and its ecosystem. The format is multiple choice questions. Questions and answers are short and concise, no tricky wording. 

Honestly, I don't think the two certs are as valuable as CKA, CKAD or CKS. It is like "talk is cheap, show me the code". Only take these if you are interested in earning the Kubestronaut title.

I did not spent much time preparing for these two after passing the 3 hands-on certs. My KodeKloud 1-month subscription was still valid after CKS, so I jumped to the KCNA and KCSA courses, and took mock exams the day before the real exams. Quite helpful, a lot of questions in the mock exams appear in the real exams.
